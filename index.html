Who is Frank Ocean?

Frank Ocean is an American singer and songwriter known for his emotional, honest music. He blends R&B, soul, and pop in a unique way. He rose to fame with Nostalgia, Ultra (2011) and won a Grammy with Channel Orange (2012). In 2016, he released Blonde, one of the most praised albums of the decade.
